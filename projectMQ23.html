PROJECT MQ23

MANNED QUADCOPTER 2023

The advancement of technology has made it possible for human beings to fly in the sky using aerial vehicles. Traditional aircrafts have been in use for many decades, but the recent trend has been the development of quadcopters, which are small, agile, and easy to maneuver. The team aims to take this technology a step further by developing a quadcopter that can be piloted by a human.

The successful completion of this project will result in the development of a manned quadcopter that can be controlled by a human pilot. The quadcopter will be able to fly at different speeds, heights, and directions, and will provide a safe and comfortable flying experience for the pilot. The development of this technology will have numerous applications in various fields, including military, search and rescue, aerial photography, and personal transportation. It will also open up new possibilities for research and development in the field of aerial robotics, and will pave the way for future innovations in the field of aerial vehicles.

AeroAstro SWUG

SolidWorks User Group Network

FEBRUARY 6, 2023

AeroAStro SWUG Bengaluru is official SWUG at the Dassult Systemes and we are a community of professionals and enthusiasts who use Solidworks, a computer-aided design (CAD) software, in various industries and fields. Our user group is dedicated to providing support, resources, and networking opportunities for all Solidworks users. Whether you are new to Solidworks or have been using it for years, you will find valuable information and connections within our community. Our members come from a wide range of backgrounds and levels of experience, making it a great place to learn and exchange ideas.

We will be hosting regular meetings, webinars, and events where members can share their knowledge, ask questions, and connect with others who use Solidworks. We also have an active online forum where members can seek help, share tips and tricks, and stay up-to-date on the latest Solidworks news and updates. In addition to technical support and professional development, our user group also offers opportunities for personal growth and career advancement. By joining our network, you will have access to job listings, resume reviews, and other career resources. We are excited to have you as part of our community and look forward to your participation and contributions. Thank you for choosing Solidworks and for joining the Solidworks User Group Network.

About us

Team AeroAstro is a new technical team atthe R.V College of Engineering that is dedicated to the research and development of manned quadcopters, also known as electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) vehicles. Our team is composed of students from various disciplines. Together, we are working to design and build a fully functional eVTOL prototype that can safely transport a pilot along with some payload. Our goal is to make air travel more efficient and accessible for everyone. We are excited to be at the forefront of this rapidly advancing field and look forward to making a significant impact in the industry.
